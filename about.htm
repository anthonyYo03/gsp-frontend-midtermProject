Who Are We ?

I am Anthony Younes, the founder of GSP (Grand Sports Club). At 21 years old, with a background in IT, I have always dreamed of creating a place where people can engage in a wide variety of sports. Mission:

Our mission is to create a welcoming environment where people can stay active, make friends, and feel happy. We aim to provide a positive atmosphere where everyone feels encouraged to stay healthy through sports and fitness.

Vision:

To become a leading sports club known for fostering community well-being, personal growth, and a passion for fitness. We envision a place where members can connect over their love for sports and find joy in staying active.

Goals :

Our goals at GSP are to promote wellness, strengthen community ties, and increase accessibility to fitness activities. We encourage a healthy lifestyle for people of all ages and provide sports programs that foster community through shared activities. Our training offerings support both beginners and advanced players, and our facilities are designed to be affordable and accessible for those interested in maintaining an active lifestyle.
